First blog in English

Jan 23, 2018 | 日常

This is my first time I write blog in English. It’s quite difficult. Thanks Sophie’s help. She give me a lot motivation to improve my writing. I hope I can keey doing these.

Recently, I’ve been trying to assemble my bicycle so I can go to the school by bike. I think it’s a good way to exercise every day. However, I’ve just noticed there are no bicycle lanes on streets in London. Most bikers ride with other vehicles in the same lane. In China, most cities have individual bicycle lines. If you ride bike on the vehicle lane, you will be stoped and fined! But, I was totally shocked after I checked up the number of fatalities resulting from road accidents in the UK and China. The gap is completely unbelievable. This report was published by the UK government in 2017. According the report, there were 1792 reported road deaths in a year in the UK. The Chinese authority hasn’t published the specific number of death from road accidents since 2013. Therefore, I only found a number from Wikipedia which was reported by WHO. It’s said there were 261,367 people dead in 2013.

These numbers really shocked me for a while. The road fatalities per 100,000 vehicles in China is 104.5. It is 20 times the UK’s number. I have to say, nothing can be more dangerous than driving in China. My graduation project was evaluating driving style. Basically, I needed to find out what is main difference between drivers and evaluated which is safer. I watched a lot of footage recorded in the research. Most drivers have variety of bad habits which will distract their attention easily, like calling, picking up something from floor, changing a CD, even replying on text messages. I had watched so many similar behaviours that I thought they were normal habits of drivers. Now, I realise these action must be the main reason of high rate of accident in China. If I have more time, I will figure out more data about that problem.
